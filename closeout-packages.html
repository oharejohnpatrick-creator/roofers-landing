How It Works

Five steps from inspection to homeowner delivery

Step 1

Final Inspection Passes

The automation detects the passed inspection — whether from a portal, email notification, or status change — and immediately triggers the closeout workflow. No one has to notice or act first.

Step 2

AI Assembles the Full Package

Warranties, job photos from CompanyCam, insurance documents, and any other required files are pulled and organized into a single Google Drive folder — automatically, without anyone lifting a finger.

Step 3

Email Drafted — Candy Already CC'd

A professional closeout email is pre-written and waiting in the inbox — correct homeowner address, all documents attached, and Candy automatically CC'd on every single one. No more oversights.

Step 4

Asana Task Created to Review & Send

Admin receives one Asana task: click the Google Drive link, confirm the documents look right, and hit send. The entire process takes under a minute — no building, no searching, no composing.

Step 5

Homeowner Receives a Polished Package

Every homeowner gets a professional, complete closeout package the same day their inspection passes. No waiting, no follow-up calls, no missing documents — just a great final impression.
