How It Works

One dashboard that pulls from every platform you already use

Step 1

Connect Your Existing Tools

The dashboard reads from MarketSharp, Roofr, QuickBooks, CompanyCam, and your permit log. No manual data entry — it pulls what's already there.

Step 2

Every Job on One Screen

See every active job with its production stage, permit status, invoice status, and outstanding AR — all in a single view that updates in real time.

Step 3

Click Through to Any Source

Every row is hyperlinked. Click a job name and jump directly into MarketSharp. Click the invoice status and land in QuickBooks. No searching, no tab-switching.

Step 4

Flags & Alerts Surface Automatically

Overdue invoices, stalled permits, and jobs stuck in a stage too long are automatically flagged — so you can manage proactively instead of reacting to problems.

Step 5

Weekly Metrics, Zero Prep

Pipeline counts, jobs by stage, and AR totals are pulled and formatted automatically for your weekly ops meeting. No manual prep, no spreadsheet copying — just open and present.
